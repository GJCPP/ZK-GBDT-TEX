\section{Generative AI Usage}\label{sec:generative-ai-usage}
The authors used generative AI tools only for language-level assistance, such as
grammar correction, spelling correction, phrasing suggestions, and improving the
readability of existing text. The protocol design, mathematical formulation,
security and soundness analysis, implementation, experiments, interpretation of
results, and all technical claims were developed, checked, and approved by the
authors. The authors take full responsibility for the content of the paper.
